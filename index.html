Bike Plans for employees

Veloretti Electric benefit both the environment and the health of employees. Oh, and they look good too. Cost-free for the employer and outside of the WKR

Discover more



Service, maintenance and insurance

The lease includes an all-in service package. Throughout the entire 36 months, you are fully insured against damage, theft and maintenance. In addition, you always have access to roadside assistance.


Bike Plans for employees

The bike plan is totally independent of the WKR and is free for you as an employer. The employee pays the lease cost and you, as the employer, decide whether you want to contribute to the costs
